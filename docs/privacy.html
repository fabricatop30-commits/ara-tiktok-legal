Privacy Policy – ARA

ARA is an internal automation tool. This policy describes what data is processed when you authorize ARA with TikTok and other platforms.

1) Data We Process

  • OAuth tokens (access token and refresh token) returned by TikTok after you authorize.
  • Basic account identifiers needed to publish content on your behalf.
  • Logs of publishing attempts (timestamps, platform, media identifiers, and responses).

2) Purpose

Data is used solely to authenticate and publish content to the user’s authorized accounts.

3) Storage

Tokens are stored locally on the user’s machine/server in a credentials folder controlled by the user.

4) Sharing

No personal data is sold. Data is only transmitted to the platform APIs required to perform publishing.

5) Contact

Privacy inquiries: privacy@pandorabox.space
